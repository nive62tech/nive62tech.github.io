Our story

A garden that
learned to brew

Leaf & Latte was born from one stubborn question — what if you could sit inside a living nursery, surrounded by a thousand plants, with a perfect cup of coffee in your hand?

The rain garden came next. The mist system. The ferns draping down the walls. And slowly, so did the regulars who now treat this corner of Koorkenchery as their second living room.

We are open every day. The coffee is always fresh. The plants are always alive. There is always room for one more.
